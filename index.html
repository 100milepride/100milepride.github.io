Pride in the Park

Pride in the Park is an all ages event taking place in the 100 Mile House Centennial Park on June 5, 2022 from 11:00 AM to 3:00 PM (PST).

Everyone is welcome to attend and support LGBTQ+ pride!

  • What's Happening?

Celebrate Pride in our Community!

Get together with your friends and family to celebrate inclusiveness and diversity at the 100 Mile House Centennial Park! Located west of Downtown, the main entrance with parking is on Cedar Avenue beside the Parkside Art Centre. Dress up, dance to the music, and have fun! Speaking of dressing up, there will be a prize for best dressed pet at the Big Gay Dog Walk!

Music and Food

Come dance to the tunes mixed by the Art of Noise music service!
Refreshments will be available, and Pizza will be served at noon!

Special Guest Abb 'Original!

We're proud to have 2-spirit drag performer Abb 'Original provide some entertainment at this event!

Fun and Activities

Take part in several fun activities, including a 'Big Gay Dog Walk' through the park (which includes a prize for the best dressed pet!), a scavenger hunt, and games!
See below for more info!

Activities and Events

The following activities and events will be taking place:

Centennial Park Scavenger Hunt with prizes

An exciting scavenger hunt with a prize draw at the end! The hunt happens between 11:00 AM and 2:00 PM, with the prize draw taking place shortly afterwards

Big Gay Dog Walk

  • Begins at 1:00 pm
  • Owners walk leashed around the park area ending at the amphitheater
  • Prize for best dressed pet
  • Judging takes place at the stage area after the walk

Ladder Toss

Ladder toss is a lawn game played by throwing bolas onto a ladder. Such fun!

Gay Croquet

The classic lawn game! Will we see the Queen of Hearts?

Spike Ball

AKA roundnet, this is a team game where players serve a ball off a trampoline-like net.

Washer Toss

This is a lawn game where players toss washers at platforms to scrore points!

Score 4

Bounce the balls off of the platform and go for 4-in-a-row to win (up and down, side to side, or diagonal)!
